# Spec Driven Development

Status: Hypothesis

## Statement
Specification-driven development produces more reliable AI-generated software than unstructured prompting.

## Assumptions
- Requirements are documented before implementation.
- Specifications remain synchronized with implementation.
- Teams follow documented processes.

## Supporting Evidence
- Structured specifications reduce ambiguity.
- Specifications improve consistency across teams.
- AI systems perform better when given clear requirements.

## Counterarguments
- Creating specifications requires additional effort.
- Small projects may not benefit significantly.
