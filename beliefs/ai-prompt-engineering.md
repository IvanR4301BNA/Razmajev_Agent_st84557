# AI Prompt Engineering

Status: Tested

## Statement
The quality of AI-generated software depends heavily on the quality and structure of the prompt.

## Assumptions
- AI systems require context.
- Clear requirements reduce ambiguity.
- Specifications improve output quality.

## Supporting Evidence
- Detailed prompts generate more predictable results.
- Structured prompts reduce hallucinations.
- Prompt engineering improves development efficiency.

## Counterarguments
- Strong models can sometimes infer missing context.
- Excessive prompt detail can slow iteration.
