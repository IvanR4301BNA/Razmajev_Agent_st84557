# SAFe Scaling Agile

Status: Hypothesis

## Statement
SAFe helps coordinate multiple Agile teams working on large software systems.

## Assumptions
- Multiple teams share dependencies.
- Large projects require coordination.
- Consistent planning improves delivery.

## Supporting Evidence
- Agile Release Trains improve alignment.
- PI Planning improves communication.
- System Demos provide integrated feedback.

## Counterarguments
- SAFe introduces additional process overhead.
- Small teams may not require SAFe practices.
