# SOLID Study Experiment

## Actions Performed
- Studied the five SOLID principles.
- Created a structured belief document about SOLID.
- Connected SOLID principles to maintainability and extensibility.

## Measurable Results
- One belief document was created.
- The principles were summarized in a reusable format.
- The topic became easier to connect with software architecture.

## Deviations From Expectation
Liskov Substitution Principle and Dependency Inversion Principle required more attention than expected.

## Unexpected Signals
The most useful principles for project structure appear to be Single Responsibility and Dependency Inversion.
