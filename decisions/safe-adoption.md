# SAFe Adoption Position

## Decision Question
Should SAFe concepts be incorporated into project management practices?

## Context Snapshot
The course introduces Agile scaling through SAFe.

## Alternatives Considered
- Pure Scrum
- Kanban
- SAFe concepts
- Waterfall

## Decision
Adopt selected SAFe concepts for learning purposes.

## Reasoning
SAFe introduces useful concepts such as PI Planning, alignment, and systems thinking.

## Expected Outcomes
- Better understanding of large-scale Agile development.
- Improved planning discipline.

## Reconsideration Conditions
If project complexity remains too low to justify additional process structure.
